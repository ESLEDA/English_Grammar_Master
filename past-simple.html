Past Simple

Pasado Simple

¿Qué es el Past Simple?

Se usa para hablar de acciones completadas en un momento específico del pasado. El tiempo exacto puede estar expresado o implícito. Es el tiempo narrativo por excelencia para contar historias.

Estructura

✓ Forma Afirmativa

Sujeto + verbo en pasado (V2 / verbo + -ed)
  • I worked yesterday.
  • You worked yesterday.
  • He/She/It worked yesterday.
  • We worked yesterday.
  • They worked yesterday.

✗ Forma Negativa

Sujeto + did + not + verbo base
  • I did not (didn't) work yesterday.
  • She didn't work yesterday.
  • They didn't work yesterday.

? Forma Interrogativa

Did + sujeto + verbo base?
  • Did you work yesterday?
  • Did she call you?
  • Did they arrive on time?

Usos del Past Simple

✅ Acción completada

Con tiempo definido en el pasado

She left at 8 AM.

📖 Narrativa

Secuencia de eventos pasados

He woke up, ate and left.

🔁 Hábito pasado

Algo que ya no ocurre

We played football every day.

⏱️ Duración pasada

Periodo ya concluido

I lived in London for 5 years.

Reglas para formar el pasado (-ed)

1. Regla general: agregar -ed

work → worked,  play → played,  walk → walked

2. Verbo termina en -e: agregar solo -d

live → lived,  love → loved,  dance → danced

3. Consonante + y → cambiar -y por -ied

study → studied,  carry → carried,  try → tried

4. CVC → doblar la consonante final + -ed

stop → stopped,  plan → planned,  drop → dropped

5. Verbos irregulares (V2)

Deben memorizarse

go → went,  have → had,  see → saw,  buy → bought

Palabras Clave

yesterday last night last week ago in 2010 when I was once then
