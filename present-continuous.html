Present Continuous

Presente Continuo

¿Qué es el Present Continuous?

Se usa para describir acciones que están ocurriendo en este momento, situaciones temporales y planes futuros ya acordados. También expresa tendencias o cambios en desarrollo.

Estructura

✓ Forma Afirmativa

Sujeto + am/is/are + verbo-ing
  • I am working right now.
  • You are working right now.
  • He/She/It is working right now.
  • We are working right now.
  • They are working right now.

✗ Forma Negativa

Sujeto + am/is/are + not + verbo-ing
  • I am not working right now.
  • You are not (aren't) working right now.
  • He/She/It is not (isn't) working right now.
  • We are not (aren't) working right now.
  • They are not (aren't) working right now.

? Forma Interrogativa

Am/Is/Are + sujeto + verbo-ing?
  • Am I working right now?
  • Are you working right now?
  • Is he/she/it working right now?
  • Are we working right now?
  • Are they working right now?

Usos del Present Continuous

⏱️ Acción en curso

Pasa exactamente ahora

She is reading a book.

📅 Plan futuro

Acuerdo o cita confirmada

I am meeting Tom tonight.

🌱 Situación temporal

No es permanente

He is living with his parents.

📈 Tendencia

Cambio o evolución en progreso

Prices are rising.

Reglas para agregar -ing

1. Regla general: agregar -ing

La mayoría de los verbos solo añaden -ing

work → working,  read → reading,  play → playing

2. Verbo termina en -e: quitar la -e y agregar -ing

Se elimina la e final antes de agregar -ing

make → making,  write → writing,  come → coming

3. CVC (consonante-vocal-consonante): doblar la consonante final

Solo si la última sílaba es tónica y el verbo termina en CVC

run → running,  sit → sitting,  begin → beginning

⚠️ Excepción: no se dobla w, x, y (show → showing, fix → fixing)

4. Verbo termina en -ie: cambiar -ie por -y antes de -ing

die → dying,  lie → lying,  tie → tying

💡 Tip

Los verbos de estado (know, love, believe, want, have…) normalmente no se usan en Continuous. Se dice "I know" y no "I am knowing".
