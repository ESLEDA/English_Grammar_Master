Past Continuous

Pasado Continuo

¿Qué es el Past Continuous?

Describe una acción que estaba en progreso en un momento específico del pasado. Se usa frecuentemente junto con el Past Simple para mostrar que una acción interrumpió otra que ya estaba ocurriendo.

Estructura

✓ Forma Afirmativa

Sujeto + was/were + verbo-ing
  • I was working at 5pm.
  • You were working at 5pm.
  • He/She/It was working at 5pm.
  • We were working at 5pm.
  • They were working at 5pm.

✗ Forma Negativa

Sujeto + was/were + not + verbo-ing
  • I was not (wasn't) sleeping at midnight.
  • They were not (weren't) studying at 10pm.

? Forma Interrogativa

Was/Were + sujeto + verbo-ing?
  • Was she working when you called?
  • Were they watching TV at 8pm?

Usos del Past Continuous

🎯 Acción en progreso

En un momento específico del pasado

At 8 PM, I was cooking dinner.

⚡ Acción interrumpida

Mientras ocurría algo más

I was reading when he called.

🔄 Acciones paralelas

Dos acciones simultáneas en el pasado

She was cooking while he was cleaning.

🎭 Ambiente narrativo

Describir el contexto de una historia

The sun was setting. Birds were singing.

Past Continuous + Past Simple

Acción larga (PC) + interrupción (PS)

El Past Continuous da el contexto; el Past Simple la interrupción

I was sleeping when the alarm rang.

Conectores clave: WHEN y WHILE

WHEN introduce la interrupción (Past Simple) · WHILE introduce la acción en progreso (Past Continuous)

She was driving while it was raining.

Palabras Clave

when while at that moment at 5pm all day as still
