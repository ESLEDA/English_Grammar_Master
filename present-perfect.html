Present Perfect

Presente Perfecto

¿Qué es el Present Perfect?

Conecta el pasado con el presente. Se usa para hablar de experiencias de vida, acciones recientes con resultado presente, o situaciones que empezaron en el pasado y continúan ahora. El momento exacto no importa.

Estructura

✓ Forma Afirmativa

Sujeto + have/has + participio pasado (V3)
  • I have worked here for 2 years.
  • You have worked here for 2 years.
  • He/She/It has worked here for 2 years.
  • We have worked here for 2 years.
  • They have worked here for 2 years.

✗ Forma Negativa

Sujeto + have/has + not + participio pasado
  • I have not (haven't) worked today.
  • He/She/It has not (hasn't) worked today.
  • They haven't worked today.

? Forma Interrogativa

Have/Has + sujeto + participio pasado?
  • Have you ever worked abroad?
  • Has she finished her homework?
  • Have they arrived yet?

Usos del Present Perfect

🌍 Experiencias de vida

Sin importar cuándo exactamente

I have visited Japan.

✅ Acción reciente

El resultado afecta el presente

She has lost her keys.

⏳ Desde / Durante

Acción que continúa hasta hoy

We have lived here since 2010.

🔁 Cuántas veces

Frecuencia hasta el momento actual

He has called three times.

FOR vs SINCE

FOR — Duración

Se usa con un período de tiempo

for two years,  for a month,  for ages

SINCE — Punto de inicio

Se usa con un momento específico en el pasado

since 2010,  since Monday,  since I was a child

Palabras Clave

already yet ever never just for since recently so far up to now
