Future Perfect

Futuro Perfecto

¿Qué es el Future Perfect?

Describe una acción que habrá sido completada antes de un momento específico en el futuro. Es el "futuro del pasado": cuando llegue ese momento futuro, la acción ya estará terminada.

Estructura

✓ Forma Afirmativa

Sujeto + will + have + participio pasado (V3)
  • I will have worked 8 hours by 6pm.
  • You will have worked 8 hours by 6pm.
  • He/She/It will have worked 8 hours by 6pm.
  • We will have worked 8 hours by 6pm.
  • They will have worked 8 hours by 6pm.

✗ Forma Negativa

Sujeto + will + not + have + participio pasado
  • I won't have finished by 5pm.
  • She will not have arrived by midnight.

? Forma Interrogativa

Will + sujeto + have + participio pasado?
  • Will you have finished the report by Friday?
  • Will they have left by then?

Usos del Future Perfect

✅ Completado antes de

Acción terminada antes de un punto futuro

By 2030, scientists will have found a cure.

🎯 Logro futuro

Meta que se alcanzará para cierta fecha

By June, I will have saved $5,000.

🔮 Suposición sobre el pasado

Asumir que algo ya habrá terminado

She will have gone home by now.

📅 Duración hasta un punto

Con expresiones de tiempo acumulado

By 2025, he will have worked here for 10 years.

Expresiones de tiempo clave

BY + tiempo → "para / antes de"

Es la expresión más frecuente con Future Perfect

by tomorrow,  by 6pm,  by the end of the year,  by then

BEFORE + evento

I will have finished before she arrives.

BY THE TIME + cláusula

By the time you read this, I will have left.

Palabras Clave

by by then by the time before already by 2030 by Friday
